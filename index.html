    HOW TO PLAY

    Follow the distance signal.

    1

    Guess any country. Search by its name or a familiar abbreviation such as UK or USA.

    2

    Read the distance. It is measured between the nearest points on both countries’ borders.

    3

    Use the trend. Every result tells you whether you moved closer or farther than your previous guess.

    4

    Find the country. Your guesses remain ranked from nearest to farthest throughout the game.

    DISTANCE MODEL

    Built for fast, consistent play.

    Distances are precompted between sampled country borders using WGS84 geodesic measurements. Shared land borders return 0 km. Overseas geometry counts where it is present in the source map.

    The interactive basemap uses OpenFreeMap data derived from OpenStreetMap. Nearer’s bundled Natural Earth country geometry remains the gameplay layer and is supplemented with mapped point approximations for microstates and small island nations.
